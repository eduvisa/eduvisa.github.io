Get Involved!

Our community relies on the hard work of hundreds of volunteers. Join our team!

Tutoring

We offer dedicated one-on-one tutoring to our community members for the SAT, ACT, AP, and IB subjects, totally free of charge! To do so, we rely on the work of over 100 volunteer tutors. In turn, we work hard to reward the dedication of our tutoring staff through curated resources, volunteer hours, social events, and a path to join our leadership team.

If you've excelled at the SAT, ACT, or any of your school subjects, and you'd like to help us continue supporting thousands of high school students through one-on-one tutoring, then we'd love to have you on our team.

Join Our Tutoring Team!

Mentorship

We know first-hand that high school and college applications can be daunting to navigate! That's why we offer mentorship for high school underclassmen in our community and seniors applying to college.

If you're a junior or senior in high school wanting to share the in's and out's of high school life and academics, or if you're a college student eager to share their college journey, we'd love to have you on our mentorship team!

Join Our Mentorship Team!
