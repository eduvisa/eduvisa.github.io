Who We Are

EduVisa is a global, student-run non-profit that aims to help students in their college and high school journey by providing a fun, interactive community of highschool and college students to help them make the most out of their high school and college experience and achieve new heights both in and out of the classroom.

How We Support Our Community

Community

We have a fun, interactive, and supportive community of students from all over the world who are excited to meet new people and talk about anything and everything from virtual learning to ice cream and everything in-between!

Tutoring

Our expert instructors provide one-on-one tutoring for all subjects from standardized tests like the SAT to World History. Our tutors, top scorers in their respective fields, are motivated and willing to help you get your dream scores!â€‹

Mentorship

In our mentorship program, college undergraduates and high school upperclassmen use their high school experience to guide fellow students to make the most out of their high school experience including essay review, resume building, scholarships, and more.
